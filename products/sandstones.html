Applications

Sandstone

Sandstone is a clastic sedimentary rock, composed of sand mineral or rock grain sized particles. Most sandstones are composed of quartz and/or feldspar because these are the most common minerals in the Earth’s crust. Owing to their natural beauty, sandstones are used for interior as well as exterior decoration including flooring, paving, cladding walls and floors.
